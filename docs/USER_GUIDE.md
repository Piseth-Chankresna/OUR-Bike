## 🚀 Getting Started

### Welcome to OUR-Bikes Store
OUR-Bikes Store is your premier destination for professional motorcycle equipment, accessories, and merchandise. Whether you're a professional rider or enthusiast, we have everything you need.

### Creating Your Account
1. **Sign Up**: Click "Login" → "Sign Up"
2. **Fill Information**: 
   - Email address
   - Secure password
   - Full name
   - Phone number
3. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox for verification
4. **Complete Profile**: Add your preferences and shipping information

### Social Login Options
- **Google**: Quick login with Google account
- **Facebook**: Connect with Facebook
- **GitHub**: Developer-friendly login option

### First Steps
1. **Browse Products**: Explore our extensive catalog
2. **Create Favorites**: Save products you love
3. **Set Preferences**: Customize your experience
4. **Enable Notifications**: Stay updated on deals

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems
- **Forgot Password**: Use password reset link
- **Account Locked**: Contact support
- **Social Login Issues**: Check app permissions
- **Verification Email**: Check spam folder

#### Payment Issues
- **Card Declined**: Check card details
- **Payment Timeout**: Refresh and retry
- **Multiple Charges**: Contact support immediately
- **Refund Status**: Track refund progress

#### Shipping Issues
- **Delayed Delivery**: Check tracking number
- **Wrong Address**: Update quickly
- **Package Damaged**: File claim immediately
- **Lost Package**: Contact carrier and support

#### Technical Issues
- **Slow Loading**: Clear browser cache
- **Images Not Loading**: Check internet connection
- **Cart Not Updating**: Refresh page
- **Mobile App Crashing**: Update app version
